Hamburger Pie


Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Potato Buds instant puffs (enough for 8 servings)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon instant minced onion
  • 3/4 cup catsup
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese (2 ounces)

Preparation:

  1. Heat oven to 350°

  2. Mix meat, 1 1/3 cups of the instant puffs (dry), the egg, salt pepper, onion, catsup and milk.

  3. Spread in ungreased pie pan, 9×1 1/2 inches.

  4. Bake uncovered 35 to 40 minutes.

  5. Prepare remaining instant puffs as directed on package for 4 servings.

  6. Top baked meat loaf with mashed potatoes; sprinkle with cheese.

  7. Bake 3 to 4 minutes longer or until cheese melts.

Storing, Freezing, and Thawing Uncooked Ground Beef

Store:

  • loosely covered, in coldest part of the refrigerator up to 24 hours (if you are planning to use the ground beef within 24 hours of purchasing).

Freeze:

  • for longer storage. Wrap one-pound packs or individual patties in freezer wrap, separating patties with double layer of freezer wrap. Label with date. Store no longer than 4 months.

Thaw:

  • wrapped 5 to 8 hours in refrigerator or cook patties frozen.
